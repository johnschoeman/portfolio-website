My Background:

Structural Engineer Turned Software Engineer.

My approach to writing software is same as my approach to life: Red Green Refactor. I like testing new ideas, iterating until my goals are surpassed and then making those solutions beautiful.

My background is in structural engineering where I was responsible for the design structural solutions for mid to high rise buildings. The design industry is also where I started my career in software development. By writing and improving software that was used by a 200 person structural engineering firm I was able to automate the design of structural members and connections used in multi-billion dollar projects. I have since switched fully into the software development space, further pursuing my interests in the engaging and sophisticated design challenges that are often presented to us involved in software.

Most recently I have been diving deep into web based technologies, including Ruby on Rails, ReactJS and GraphQL. I have contributed to successful projects built in both mobile and desktop environments.
